Where Rock Mechanics meets the Energy Challenge

The Applied Rock Mechanics and Energy Laboratory (ARMEL) at IIT Bhubaneswar is a research group dedicated to advancing the understanding of rock behaviour in the context of energy production, geological storage, and geohazard mitigation.

Our interdisciplinary approach combines experimental rock physics, advanced imaging (micro-CT, SEM), acoustic emission monitoring, and numerical modelling to address real-world engineering and environmental challenges.

Research

Experimental, numerical, and field-based investigations across rock mechanics, energy geoscience, and geo-environmental engineering.

Core Research Areas
01

Rock Mechanics & Fracture

Fracture toughness, acoustic emission monitoring, and failure mechanisms of rocks under uniaxial, triaxial, and dynamic loading, relevant to underground excavation and mining.

02

Geological Carbon Sequestration

CO2 storage in basalt and coal formations, cryogenic fracturing with liquid nitrogen, carbonate mineralisation, and long-term reservoir characterisation.

03

Unconventional Energy Resources

Enhanced coalbed methane (ECBM), shale gas, and CO2-EOR. Experimental and numerical evaluation of adsorption behaviour and formation properties.

04

Digital Rock Physics & Micro-CT

High-resolution X-ray micro-CT imaging and digital rock modelling to characterise pore networks, micro-fractures, and reservoir properties.

05

Thermal & Fluid–Rock Interaction

Effects of elevated temperature and brine saturation on rock strength, deformation, and microstructure. Implications for geothermal and CCS applications.

06

Geohazard & Slope Stability

Field investigation, hazard zonation, and numerical modelling of slope failures along highways and railway corridors. Geotechnical characterisation for infrastructure safety.

Professional Services

Expert consultancy and advisory services drawing on ARMEL’s experimental capabilities and research expertise.

⛏️

Rock Stability & Failure Risk Assessment

Expert geotechnical investigations, slope stability analysis, and remedial measures for highways, railways, and tunnels. Includes site characterisation, numerical modelling, and failure risk quantification for mining operations.

🔥

CBM / Shale Gas Formation Suitability

Laboratory and field rock mechanics testing, gas adsorption studies, and geomechanical characterisation of coal seams and shale formations to evaluate suitability for coalbed methane or shale gas extraction. Includes pore structure analysis using micro-CT and low-pressure gas adsorption.

🌿

Site Selection for Geological Carbon Sequestration (CCS)

Technical support for CCS projects: reservoir suitability assessment, caprock integrity evaluation, geomechanical CO2 injection modelling, monitoring framework design, and long-term risk analysis for storage in basalt, saline aquifers, and coal formations.

💥

Geomechanical Advisory for Induced Rock Fracturing

Consultancy on hydraulic fracturing design, fracture propagation modelling, and assessment of induced seismicity hazards. Applicable to unconventional resource extraction, geothermal well stimulation, and waste injection operations.

🌡️

Geothermal Rock Suitability & Thermal Damage Evaluation

Assessment of rock thermal properties, thermally-induced micro-cracking, and mechanical degradation under elevated temperatures. Enables informed site selection and engineering design for geothermal projects and high-temperature underground storage systems.

🔭

Digital Rock Modelling & Image-Based Rock Analysis

High-resolution micro-CT scanning, SEM imaging, and digital rock physics modelling to quantify pore networks, mineral distribution, fracture geometry, and permeability. Supports reservoir simulation, well completion optimisation, and CO2 trapping mechanism studies.

📐

Adsorption Behaviour Interpretation for Reservoir Screening

Characterisation of gas adsorption isotherms (CO2, CH4, N2) in coals and shales using low-pressure volumetric and gravimetric methods. Supports selection of optimal injection strategies for ECBM, shale gas, and CO2 sequestration projects.

🏭

Coal Quality & Environmental Risk Assessment

Geochemical, petrographic, and mechanical characterisation of coal samples for quality evaluation. Includes assessment of environmental risks associated with coal mining, ash disposal, and acid mine drainage.

✅

Expert Review & Validation of Experimental / Field Data

Independent technical review and validation of rock mechanics testing programmes, geotechnical investigation reports, and experimental datasets. Ensures compliance with ISRM and ASTM standards; expert opinions for regulatory submissions or dispute resolution.

🎓

Expert Training, Workshops & Advisory Services

Tailored professional training and workshops for industry personnel on rock mechanics, geomechanics, acoustic emission monitoring, digital rock physics, and energy geosciences. Short courses and hands-on laboratory training available on request.
